Discord Server Rules

Please follow these rules to ensure a positive experience for everyone:

Be Respectful: Treat everyone with respect and kindness. Harassment, hate speech, bullying, or any form of discrimination will not be tolerated.
No Spamming or Flooding: Avoid excessive posting, flooding chats with repetitive messages, or spamming emojis, images, or links.
Keep Content Appropriate: Ensure all content shared, including messages, images, videos, and links, is appropriate for all ages. No NSFW (Not Safe For Work) content is allowed.
No Advertising: Do not promote other Discord servers, Minecraft servers, or external products/services without prior permission from server staff.
Use Appropriate Channels: Post content in the relevant channels. Off-topic discussions should be kept to a minimum, and conversations should stay on-topic.
No Cheating or Exploits: Do not use cheats, hacks, exploits, or any unfair advantages in Minecraft gameplay. This includes but is not limited to, X-ray texture packs, duplication glitches, or fly hacks.
Respect Server Staff: Follow instructions from server moderators and administrators. If you have a concern or question, address it respectfully through direct messages or appropriate channels.
No Personal Information: Do not share personal information about yourself or others. This includes but is not limited to addresses, phone numbers, emails, or social media profiles.
Keep Conversations Civil: Engage in constructive discussions and avoid heated arguments or flame wars. If a disagreement arises, handle it respectfully or take it to private messages.
Listen to Warnings: If you receive a warning from a staff member, take it seriously and adjust your behavior accordingly. Ignoring warnings may result in further action, such as temporary or permanent bans.
